Our Product

EcoForm Olive™ is a sustainable packaging solution developed by transforming discarded olive pits into a flexible, protective, and compostable material. The product is designed to replace plastic fillers and other unsustainable packaging alternatives commonly used in shipping and storage.

Olive pits are a natural by-product of olive oil and food production. In most cases, this waste is discarded or burned. EcoForm Olive™ repurposes this material, extending its life cycle and reducing environmental harm.

Through research, experimentation, and material testing, our team has developed a packaging solution that balances sustainability, durability, and practicality.

  • Plastic-free and fully compostable
  • Produced from recycled olive pit waste
  • Lightweight yet effective for protection
  • Reduces landfill waste and CO₂ emissions
  • Designed for modern, sustainable supply chains

Why EcoForm Olive™?

EcoForm Olive™ was created to demonstrate how innovation and sustainability can work together. The project focuses on reducing plastic waste while promoting the reuse of natural resources already available within Cyprus.

Our approach reflects modern economic thinking centred on circular economy principles, where materials are reused, repurposed, and kept in circulation for as long as possible.

Sustainability Impact

EcoForm Olive™ contributes to a more sustainable future by reducing plastic dependency and repurposing agricultural waste.

By utilising olive pits — a natural by-product — the project supports responsible resource management, lowers environmental pollution, and encourages businesses to adopt eco-friendly packaging solutions.
